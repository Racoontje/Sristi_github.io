About Her

She is shy, but also energetic.. She is afraid of love so that’s why giving her reassurance, love, patience, positivity, and effort should be at least bare minimum! I will love her in the ways she have always wanted! The ways she have always deserved in the means that leaves no trace of doubt in her beautiful mind that it is home! I will be her sun to brighten her day and when the night falls I will be her moon to brighten her in the her darkest moment! I never feel like I’m wasting time with her. We could sit in silence for hours and it would still feel so full. I’m so thankful for her!

Poems for my lady

Your presence in my life feels like a dream come true. You bring joy, love, and excitement, filling my days with happiness and my nights with warmth. Your laughter is a symphony of delight, and your touch sends shivers down my spine, making me feel alive in every moment we spend together.

You are like a breathtaking work of art, an exquisite masterpiece that leaves me in awe every time I see you. Your beauty, both inside and out, captivates my heart and soul, and I find myself falling for you again and again, with each passing day.

I want you because I know I can handle everything that comes with you. I want to make you happy smile, and laugh. I want to be the one person you can trust and have a shoulder to cry on when no one else can. I want to show how perfect you are. I want to kiss your insecurities and show you feel how beautiful you really are. I love you. I only want you, no one else. I promise you!
